What is Acronymble?

Acronymble is a unique crossword puzzle where each answer is an acronym formed from the first letters of words in famous quotes from America's founding era and other historical figures.

For example, if the clue is "Opening Line of the Declaration of Independence, written by Thomas Jefferson", the answer would be "WHTTTBSTAMACE" (from "We hold these truths to be self-evident, that all men are created equal").

This puzzle combines knowledge of historical quotes with the challenge of a crossword puzzle format, creating a unique and engaging experience for history enthusiasts and puzzle lovers alike.

How It Was Created

The Acronymble puzzle was created by collecting famous quotes from America's founding era and other historical periods. Each quote was converted into an acronym by taking the first letter of each word.

These acronyms were then arranged in a crossword grid, with intersecting letters creating connections between different quotes. The grid was designed to maximize the number of acronyms while maintaining a classic crossword puzzle format.

The final puzzle features quotes from historical figures such as Thomas Jefferson, Benjamin Franklin, George Washington, and many others, providing a glimpse into the wisdom of the past through an engaging puzzle format.

How to Play

  1. Read the clue, which references a historical quote and its author
  2. Determine the original quote (or make your best guess)
  3. Take the first letter of each word in the quote to form the acronym
  4. Fill in the acronym in the crossword grid
  5. Use intersecting letters to help solve other clues

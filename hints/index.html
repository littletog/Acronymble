Hints and Tips

Stuck on the Acronymble puzzle? Here are some hints and tips to help you solve it without giving away the answers completely.

Beginner Hints

  • Start with the shorter acronyms, as they're usually easier to solve
  • Look for common words that might start with the letters you have
  • Remember that the acronyms include ALL words, including small ones like "a", "the", and "of"
  • Use the intersecting letters to help narrow down possibilities

Intermediate Hints

  • For clue #1 (across), think about the most famous line from the Declaration of Independence
  • For clue #345 (across), the quote is about always having enough of something important
  • For clue #32 (across), this is about how knowledge leads to success
  • For clue #7 (down), this is a single-word quote about a fundamental virtue
